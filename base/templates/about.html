How This Idea Came

When it was announced that Huawei Academy was to be launched at Baraton Campus. The Academy Patrons began registering the fully registered students with huawei Id's, However, The developer (Wanga Evans) saw the process as inefficient since thy were using google sheets. Some had to wait for permission inorder to update their details on the sheets. Evans thought, He could make it better by creating a simple web app that could handle the process.
